Subdivisions

Naval Special Warfare Development Group (DEVGRU)

DEVGRU is the Navy's tier one Joint Special Operations Command unit

Naval Education Training Command (NETC)

The mission of NETC is to shape the next generation of Navy personnel, making them even better than those who came before. We are dedicated to pushing the limits, ensuring that the Navy reaches its full potential and continues to excel in every way.

Office of Naval Intelligence (ONI)

ONI is the Internal Affairs division of the Navy and they are in charge of making sure every member of the Navy is held accountable for their actions.

Naval Air Force (NAF)

NAF is the Navy's Fighter Jet and Helicopter division.

Naval Corpsman (NCM)

NCM is the badass tactical medics that attach with QRFs and some low level JSOC operations.
