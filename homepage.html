About Us

We are more than just ships and sailors. We are a dedicated force of men and women who are committed to serving our country and making a difference in the world. We provide humanitarian aid, disaster relief, and peacekeeping operations around the globe.

Our Mission

The mission of the Navy is to maintain, train and equip combat-ready Naval forces capable of winning wars, deterring aggression and maintaining freedom of the seas.
